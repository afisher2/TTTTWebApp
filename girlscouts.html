What We Do

We are a FTC team from Eagan High School and we are looking to meet with Girl Scouts looking to get their robotics or STEM badges! We have a curriculum to teach the Scouts about FIRST and subsets of robotics like building and coding. We do multiple badges and our services are free. Contact us to set up a meeting!
