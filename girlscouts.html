Girl Scout Badges

We meet with Girl Scouts to help them them earn STEM badges. Our goals are to inspire girls, share our knowledge, and have fun! We offer 7 badges that we can adapt to any level. The sessions are free and can be in person or virtual.

Please use this form to share your troops’ interests and set up a meeting.

We have been doing the badges virtually, but let us know if you’re interested in doing them in person.
